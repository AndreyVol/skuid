--- layout: main title: skuid.label --- skuid.label Object ================= ## METHODS ##### `parse( mergeString, leftSide, rightSide )` Parses a string @mergeString that contains merges delimited on the left by @leftSide (e.g. '{% literal %}{{{% endliteral %}') and on the right side by @rightSide (e.g. '{% literal %}{{{% endliteral %}') and returns two arrays: the first containing the merges (the replacement values) and the second containing the formats (text in between merges). ##### `read( labelName, defaultValue )` Returns the Custom Label named @labelName, (optionally) returning @defaultValue if no such label exists
